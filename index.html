The process

How it works

Four steps from enquiry to a delivered test suite — with no surprises at any stage.

01 — SCOPE REVIEW

We understand your flows

We capture the workflows and expected behaviours provided by your team as a lightweight scoping reference before automation begins.

02 — FIXED QUOTE

You know the cost upfront

We propose a fixed price based on your Workflow count. No hourly estimates, no scope creep, no surprises. You approve before we begin.

03 — SPRINT DELIVERY

Senior SDETs build your suite

Clean Playwright code, Page Object Model architecture, resilient selectors — delivered in a defined sprint to your agreed timeline.

04 — YOU OWN IT

We hand over native Playwright TypeScript or Python. No proprietary platform, no ongoing licence. It's yours to run, extend, and own forever.
